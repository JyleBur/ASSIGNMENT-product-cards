Cat Wanted: Mittens

Mittens the mischievous black and white cat couldn't resist the temptation of an open window in a grand mansion one day. Once inside, she played with the valuable treasures she found, knocking over vases and playing with baubles. Unbeknownst to Mittens, the mansion's owner had a sophisticated security system in place and the police were alerted when she triggered an alarm. Mittens was quickly apprehended and became the talk of the town, with everyone whispering about the "burglar cat."

Cat Wanted: Whiskers

Whiskers was a feisty orange tabby who loved to cause trouble. One day, while exploring the neighborhood, he stumbled upon a group of birds sitting on a windowsill. Without hesitation, Whiskers pounced on the birds and chased them away, causing a commotion. The owner of the birds called the authorities, and soon enough, Whiskers became wanted for his actions. Despite his mischievous ways, Whiskers was still loved by the neighborhood for his playful and curious nature.
